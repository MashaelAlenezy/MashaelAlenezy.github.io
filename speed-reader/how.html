Try the speed reader

How it works

One word at a time. Smooth focus. With optional audio to help with correct pronunciation.

  • 📚 Choose a Unit and a Text
  • 🔊 Enable Text to Speech if you would like to hear the words
  • Choose a voice you prefer
  • ✨ Tap Start to begin the flasher.
  • ⬅️➡️ Tap left/right to step words; space to pause.
💡 Pro tip: Start slower, then nudge up WPM until it feels effortless.
